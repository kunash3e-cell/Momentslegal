Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: 9 June 2026

What is Moments?

Moments is a private photo journal app that helps you capture daily memories, build a personal timeline, create recaps, and stay connected with selected people.

Is Moments a social media app?

No. Moments is designed to be private. There is no public feed by default. Your memories are meant for you and only the people you choose to connect with.

Who can see my photos?

Your photos are private by default. Some content may be visible to people you choose to connect with or share with through Moments features.

Can I delete my account?

If account deletion is available in the app, you can request deletion from inside the app. You can also contact us by email for help with account or data deletion.

Does Moments have paid features?

Moments may offer paid subscriptions, premium features, storage upgrades, or other paid products. Payment and subscription management may be handled by Google Play, Apple App Store, RevenueCat, or another payment provider.

How do reminders work?

If you allow notifications, Moments may send reminders for daily prompts, recaps, connection activity, or other app-related alerts.

Are my memories backed up?

Moments may store your memories using secure cloud storage. However, you should keep your own backup of important photos or memories.
