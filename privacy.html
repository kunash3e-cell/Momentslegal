Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 June 2026

Moments is a private photo journal app designed to help users capture daily memories, build personal timelines, create recaps, and stay connected with selected people. This Privacy Policy explains how Moments collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create or use an account, we may collect information such as your name, email address, profile photo, login provider information, and user ID. If you sign in using Google, we receive basic account information provided by Google.

Photos and Memories

Moments allows you to upload photos and memory content. This may include daily photos, captions, timestamps, recap content, and information connected to your personal timeline.

Connections and Groups

If you use connection features, we may collect information about your connections, invites, accepted connections, labels, shared moments, and group connections.

Device and App Information

We may collect basic device and app information such as device type, operating system, app version, crash logs, diagnostics, and usage activity to keep the app working properly.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, we may collect and use notification tokens to send reminders, daily prompts, connection updates, delivery of recap notifications, and other app-related alerts.

Subscription and Purchase Information

If Moments offers paid features, we may collect subscription status, purchase history, entitlement status, and related billing information through app stores or subscription service providers. We do not directly collect or store your full payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your Moments account
  • Store and display your private memories
  • Show your timeline and recap content
  • Enable one-on-one and group connection features
  • Send app reminders and notifications
  • Manage subscriptions and paid features
  • Improve app performance, safety, and reliability
  • Fix bugs, prevent abuse, and protect user accounts
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How Your Photos and Memories Are Handled

Your photos and memories are intended to be private. They are not publicly posted by default. Content may only be visible to you or to people you choose to connect with through Moments features.

You are responsible for the photos and content you upload. Do not upload content that violates laws, another person's rights, or the rights of any third party.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate Moments, such as:

  • Authentication providers, such as Google Sign-In
  • Database, storage, and backend providers, such as Supabase
  • Subscription and purchase management providers, such as RevenueCat
  • App stores, such as Google Play or Apple App Store
  • Analytics, crash reporting, or diagnostic tools if used in the app

These providers may process information only as needed to provide their services, comply with legal requirements, prevent fraud, or protect the security of the app.

5. Third-Party Services

Moments may use third-party services including, but not limited to:

  • Google Sign-In for account login
  • Supabase for authentication, database, and storage
  • RevenueCat for subscription and entitlement management
  • Google Play Billing or Apple In-App Purchase for payments

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies. RevenueCat states that apps using RevenueCat may need to disclose purchase-related information such as purchase history or purchases in app store privacy forms.

6. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information using technical and organizational safeguards. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

Your data may be stored on secure cloud servers provided by our backend and storage providers.

7. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide Moments, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

If you delete your account or request deletion, we will take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize your personal information, unless we are required to keep it for legal, security, or fraud prevention reasons.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of your personal information. You may also be able to object to or restrict certain processing of your data.

You can contact us to request help with your personal information.

9. Children’s Privacy

Moments is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

10. International Users

If you use Moments from outside the country where our service providers operate, your information may be transferred to and processed in other countries. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of Moments after changes means you accept the updated policy.
